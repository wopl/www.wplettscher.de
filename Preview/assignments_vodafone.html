Vehicle Communication Concepts

Involvement

09/2014 - 01/2015

Customer

Automotive

Role

Project Manager Technical Workstream

All car manufacturers are currently evaluating new technologies for the next generation infrastructure to communicate from and into the car itself and on the other side allowing passengers to access voice and data services from the car. Additional mobile equipment (cellphones, tablets, communicating ECUs) as well as new car production processes (Faraday cage) require new concepts to fulfill all todays and future requirements. Together with our customer EE department we evaluated multiple possible solutions both from a technical as well as from a business perspective. I built the bridge communicating technical concepts with our customer and coordinating the technical discussions, papers and presentations within the wider Vodafone group.

Mobile Applications

Involvement

07/2014 - 08/2014

Customer

Manufacturing / Sales Support Tool

Role

IT-Architect / Program Manager

For a highly configurable industrial B2B product our customer requested to optimize the sales process. Instead of calculating service costs in the backoffice with 3 weeks lead time, a mobile device approach was intended to do a granular pre-estimation of service costs just in front of the customer within few seconds only.
I was building all architecture and technical design to implement such a solution. A solid prototype for selected salesforce built within a short period of time was able to demonstrate all advantages compared to the established sales and cost calculation process.
